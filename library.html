Working Hours

  • 9.30 a.m.-5.00 p.m. on all weekdays

  • 10.00 a.m.-4.00 p.m. on Saturdays

  • The library is closed on Sundays and all public holidays.

Library Rules

  • All students and staff are members of the library.

  • The no of books issued to a PG student will not be more than 5 and to an UG more than 3 at a time.

  • General books are issued for a period of two weeks.

  • Members of the teaching staff may borrow up to ten books at a time and they have to return it within one month.

  • Reference books will not be issued.

  • A book may be re-issued to the same user if there is no other applicant for it.

  • The Librarian can recall a book at any time.

  • A fine of Rs .1/- for general books will be levied per day if a book is retained beyond the permitted period.

  • Absence from the Institute will not be considered as an excuse for delay in returning the books.

  • Current periodicals will be issued for use in the library.

  • On no account shall a member spoil or disfigure a book. If a book is lost,they shall pay 3 times the price of the book.

  • Students must return their “Reader’s Tickets” when leaving the Institute and should obtain “NO DUES CERTIFICATE”.
