Objectives

  • To develop a system for conscious, consistent and catalytic action to improve the academic and administrative performance of the institution.

  • To promote measures for institutional functioning towards quality enhancement and sustenance through internalization of quality culture and institutionalization of best practices.

  • To attain the vision of the institute through executing, channelizing the strategic plan.

Functions

  • Development and application of quality benchmarks parameters through periodical conduct of Academic and Administrative Audit to promote quality and accountability.

  • Facilitating the creation of a learner-centric environment conducive to quality education and faculty development to adopt the required knowledge and technology for participatory teaching and learning process.

  • Collection and analysis of feedback from all stakeholders on quality-related institutional processes.

  • Dissemination of information on various quality parameters of higher education.

  • Organize workshops and seminars on quality related themes and promotion of quality circles.

  • Develop an organised methodology of documentation leading to quality improvement.

  • Integrate quality related activities in the institution including adoption and dissemination of good practices.

  • Annual Quality Assurance Report (AQAR) preparation and submission.

Value Framework

To promote the following core values among the HEIs of the country:

  • Contributing to National Developmen

  • Fostering Global Competencies among Students

  • Inculcating a Value System among Students

  • Promoting the Use of Technology

  • Quest for Excellence
