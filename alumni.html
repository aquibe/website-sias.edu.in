OSRA - Alumni of SIAS

SAFI Alumni Association - OSRA is a non-profit initiative founded in 2019 (Registered as a charitable society) to embrace the vision and mission of the Social Advancement Foundation of India. Our alumni have unfolded globally and made their Alma Mater proud by excelling as educators, supervisors, researchers, administrators, entrepreneurs, and technocrats who have achieved greatness in their respective fields and are making substantial contributions to socioeconomic advancement within and outside our country. The Executive Committee of OSRA comprises a squad of enthusiastic individuals whose key emphasis is to build a steady network of our renowned alumni working among diverse sectors and fields via special programs and events held throughout the year. SAFI has always deemed its alumni as a source of support and sustenance in its ambitions of constructive nation-building through quality higher education and research in multi-disciplinary subjects. With the strong support and enthusiastic involvement of the SAFI Management, Principal, Faculty, Staff, and Students, the alumni association has carried out various events which provide career-development programs, scholarships, sponsorships, collaborations, financial aid to the deprived, etc. OSRA Alumni Association has been pervasively bringing together former students and constructing their progressive camaraderie with the institution throughout the past years and we will continue to do so.
