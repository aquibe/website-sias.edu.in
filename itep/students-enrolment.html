Students' Enrolment

The proposed Integrated Teacher Education Programme (ITEP), namely B.A. B.Ed. (Secondary) – 1 Unit (Annual Intake: 50 Students) and B.Sc. B.Ed. (Secondary) – 1 Unit (Annual Intake: 50 Students), are yet to be approved by the National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE). As the approval process is still in progress, admissions to these programmes have not yet commenced. All admission procedures will adhere strictly to the norms and standards set by the NCTE and the affiliating university.
