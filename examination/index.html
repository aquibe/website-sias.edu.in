Office of the Controller of Examination

The Office of the Controller of Examinations at Safi Institute of Advanced Study was launched in the year 2024, is a vital statutory body in an autonomous college, ensuring the smooth and transparent conduct of all examinations. Established in accordance with UGC guidelines, the office is responsible for planning, organizing, and executing examinations systematically, maintaining academic integrity and excellence.

The CoE office operates under the leadership of the Controller of Examinations, reporting to the Principal, and is supported by a dedicated team of deputies and clerical staff. The office oversees the entire examination process, from scheduling and question paper setting to evaluation and result publication, strictly adhering to the Examination Calendar set at the beginning of each academic year.

The examinations office maintains a grievance redressal mechanism as a support to the students to deal with examination-related grievances.
