Welcome to SAFI

SAFI Institute of Advanced Study (Autonomous), one of the premier Higher Education Institutions in the state of Kerala was established in 2005 by the Social Advancement Foundation of India (SAFI), a Non-profitable Charitable Trust. It offers 2 Phd , 2 ITEP (Secondary Programmes), 15 UG and 8 PG programmes in Arts, Science, Commerce, Management , Media studies and Artificial Intelligence. It is affiliated to University of Calicut and recognised by Government of Kerala and University Grants Commission (UGC) under 2(f).The college has been granted autonomy by UGC for 10 years from 2024-2025 to 2033-34.
