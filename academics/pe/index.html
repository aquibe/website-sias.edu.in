Dept. of Physical Education

The Department of physical education, SAFI Institute of Advanced Study established in the year 2005, Department offers open course in Physical Education for undergraduate students in the fifth semester. The open course “Physical activity, Health and wellness” intends to provide awareness in physical fitness to the students. Department keeping good physical fitness also as important as nurturing the brain with intellectual activities. SIAS emphasises on the need to participate in sports and games and other physical activities.
