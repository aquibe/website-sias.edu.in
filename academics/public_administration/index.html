Dept. of Public Administration

Our department is dedicated to preparing the next generation of public administrators to tackle complex societal issues and serve their communities with effectiveness and integrity. Public Administration is gaining importance as a subject in the UPSC civil service examination. A career in Civil Services offers young people the opportunity to serve their country in diverse and challenging roles, making a positive impact on society despite facing various obstacles. Moreover, the discipline of Public Administration serves as the foundation for aspiring civil servants in their future roles, and it also offers opportunities in the private sector beyond the realm of civil service.


Vision

To become a leading center of excellence in public administration education and research. We envision a future where students are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to address complex and pressing societal issues, such as inequality, sustainability, and governance challenges.


Mission

  • Provide students with a broad understanding of the principles, theories, and practices of public administration, allowing them to develop the analytical and decision-making skills needed to address public policy issues effectively.

  • Collaborate with community organizations and government agencies to address local and regional challenges, building strong partnerships that benefit both the students and the community.

  • Encourage interdisciplinary collaboration, enabling students to draw on the insights and perspectives of multiple disciplines to address complex social problems.

  • Contribute to the advancement of knowledge and practice in public administration, through research, publication, and dissemination of new ideas and best practices.

  • Offer personalized academic advising and mentoring that helps students identify and achieve their educational and career goals.
