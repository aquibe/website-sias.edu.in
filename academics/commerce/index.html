Dept. of Commerce

Department of Commerce, SIAS College is the most renowned and largest department. The Department of Commerce offers B.com (Finance and Islamic Finance) at undergraduate level along with M.com at the postgraduate level. The department provides experienced faculty which has over the years ensured a smooth and vibrant teaching -learning experience. The Department aims at providing insight into several fields like finance, banking, law, insurance, e-commerce, accounting, taxation, management, international business etc. which play an important role in today's business world. The Department supports students to aim for professional courses like C.A, I.C.W.A./A.C.C.A., and C.S and has signed MoU with ISDC,U.K. The Department is proud to have its Alumni occupying prestigious positions in the corporate as well as public sectors.
