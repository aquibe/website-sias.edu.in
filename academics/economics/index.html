Dept. of Economics

The graduate programme in Economics was commenced in the year 2009. Economics is an important discipline both career and job wise. Well-trained economists are in huge demand not only in India but also at the global scenario.

The three-month research projects, taken up during their final semester help the students to put their knowledge to use and sharpen their skills. The Department organises guest lectures by eminent scholars from reputed Institutes to provide exposure in different areas. Field visits to Industries and Institutes are arranged.
