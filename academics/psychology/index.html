Dept. of Psychology

The Department of Psychology established on 2020. The programme gives its students a strong foundation in Psychology. Psychology is the scientific study of behaviour and mental processes.

The scope of Psychology involves : Clinical Psychology, Counselling Psychology, Cognitive Psychology, Cross – cultural Psychology, Cultural Psychology, Developmental Psychology , Educational Psychology, Environmental Psychology, Evolutionary Psychology, Experimental Psychology, Health Psychology, Positive Psychology, School Psychology, Sports Psychology etc.
