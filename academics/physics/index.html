Dept. of Physics

Department of Physics was established in the year 2015. The undergraduate program in physics started in 2021. Physics is natural science which deals with properties of matter and energy. The department has a well furnished and spacious laboratory with modern equipment meeting the requirements of curriculum.

The BSc physics program helps students to have a strong foundation in classical and modern physics. It gives students basic knowledge in computer programming and numerical analysis. Students will also be able to apply and verify theoretical concepts through laboratory experiments.


Vision

Establish a centre of excellence in physics, grooming scholars having vision and leadership qualities, thus contributing to the holistic development of the society.


Mission

  • Provide cutting edge technologies and innovative methods in teaching and learning of Physics.

  • Facilitate research in physics and interdisciplinary areas by offering state of the art laboratories.
