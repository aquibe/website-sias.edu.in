Dept. of Microbiology

The Department of Microbiology, started in the year 2005 with a 3 year B.Sc programme and currently offers both B.Sc and M.Sc programme in Microbiology. The Department has young enthusiastic and well qualified faculty members who are actively involved in providing quality education, inculcating research aptitude and creating an excellent research environment through its ongoing programmes.

The Department aims to impart quality education to students, prepare them for new research challenges and to create and foster technology for the upliftment of the society. Microbiology forms the earliest and the most advanced area of Life Sciences. Here at SAFI Institute of Advanced Study, we aim to produce microbiologists of excellent caliber, good laboratory and technical skills. The Department is well equipped with all the necessary instruments and research facilities


Vision

To provide quality education and training for the undergraduate and graduate students to become future leaders in science, medicine and industry, to promote research that advances the frontiers of knowledge in the field of microbiology.


Mission

To be a recognized Institute specialized in Microbiology and Bioscience research of national and international importance.
