Dept. of Islamic Studies

M.A. Islamic Studies has been one of the postgraduate programmes offered by the Institute since 2006. Islamic Studies attempts to study and promote research on all aspects of the Islamic civilization and culture from its origin to the present, irrespective of the geographical boundaries.

The syllabus covers Major World Religions and Comparative Religious Studies. Seminars and Workshops are organised for students to augment the knowledge gained from the curriculum.
