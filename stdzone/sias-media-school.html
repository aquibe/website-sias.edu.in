SIAS Media School

SIAS Media School is a unique media institute. Being a state-of- art department of Mass Communication and Journalism, at the prestigious SAFI institute of Advanced Study, Vazhayoor, we train our students to become professionally competent and academically eminent. SIAS offers a two year post graduation programme competent and academically eminent. SIAS offers a two year post graduation programme in mass communication and journalism under the university of Calicut. In a short time, SIAS Media School has become the much-sought destination of aspiring media students from various corners. We provide industry fitness through professional exposure and various rigorous training methods, our alternative training methods are aimed at building a new generation of media professionals, with conviction, commitment and competence.
