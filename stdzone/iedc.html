Innovation Entrepreneurship Development Centre

The main aim of Innovation Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC SIAS) is to develop institutional mechanism to create & promote innovations and entrepreneurial skills among the students. It also aims to foster techno-entrepreneurship for generation of wealth and employment in various sectors of Science and Humanities.

This Cell nurtures innovative thinking and becoming a facilitator between the students and the industries outside by acting as a hub of contacts and network that helps in finding the potential entrepreneurs the right people to approach for any task.
