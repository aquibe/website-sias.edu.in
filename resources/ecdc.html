E-CONTENT DEVELOPMENT CENTRE

Safi institute of advanced study has created an E content development Centre with several objectives. In order to accomplish some important objectives like Generation of e-content in all subjects ,to make teachers of SIAS experts in e-content creation and also to development of advanced e-content and make it available those content to students of various wards ,through different delivery modes for formal education, SIAS has formulated a powerful team including faculties from different areas like computer science, Biology Science, Literature ,Journalism etc,also collaborated support from technologists and media managers of SIAS. ECDC is a part of digital hub of SIAS, a digital ecosystem which has launched as an auxiliary method for physical teaching and learning process. It can provide digital assistance for teachers for providing students with advanced e-contents and thereby make the teaching learning process more fruitful
